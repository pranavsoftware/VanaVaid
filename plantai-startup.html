VanaVaid

AI-powered plant intelligence that identifies wild & medicinal plants, reveals their healing properties, and guides you to prepare natural remedies — for everyone.

The Problem

Billions of plants. Almost no one knows what they are.

Centuries of plant medicine knowledge is locked in books, elder communities, and expensive experts — while 80% of the world's population relies on herbal medicine without proper guidance.

🌾

Unknown Plants Everywhere

People encounter medicinal plants daily in forests, fields, and gardens but have no way to identify them or understand their benefits and risks.

⚠️

Risk of Misuse

Wrong preparation of medicinal plants can be toxic. Without accurate guidance, self-medication with herbs can cause serious harm.

📚

Knowledge is Fragmented

Ayurvedic, TCM, African, and Indigenous botanical knowledge exists in silos — never accessible to the common person in one unified platform.

💊

Expensive Healthcare

In developing nations, millions of people lack access to affordable medicine and turn to plants — but without safe, reliable guidance.

The Solution

One app. Point. Identify. Heal.

VanaVaid is an AI platform that combines computer vision, a massive medicinal plant database, and language AI to turn any smartphone into a botanical doctor's assistant.

📸 Snap & Identify

Point your camera at any plant — AI identifies the species in seconds with 95%+ accuracy.

🧬 Medicinal Property Engine

Get a detailed breakdown of chemical compounds, therapeutic uses, and health benefits.

🫖 Remedy Preparation Guide

Step-by-step instructions: how to prepare teas, pastes, extracts, or poultices safely.

🚫 Safety Warnings

Drug interactions, toxicity alerts, pregnancy warnings — built right in for every plant.

🌍 Multi-Tradition Knowledge

💬 AI Chatbot Assistant

"What plant helps with a headache near me?" — conversational queries answered instantly.

How It Works

From unknown leaf to trusted remedy

1

Capture the Plant

User takes a photo of any plant — in the wild, garden, market, or their kitchen. Works with leaves, flowers, bark, seeds, and roots.

2

AI Vision Identifies It

Our fine-tuned computer vision model (trained on 500,000+ plant images) identifies the species. Multiple angles increase confidence score.

3

Knowledge Engine Activates

Pulls from our proprietary database of 10,000+ medicinal plants — covering properties, active compounds, and regional uses across traditions.

4

Personalised Remedy Guide

Based on the user's health concern (e.g. cold, inflammation, digestion), the AI delivers a specific, safe preparation method with dosage guidance.

5

Expert Review Layer

Ayurvedic doctors and botanists review AI outputs quarterly. Users can flag for human expert review for complex or rare cases.
